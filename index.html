A: NPS stands for Net Promoter Score. This is a rating system between 1-10 of customer sastifaction with 10 being the top score.

* Any score between 1-6 is classed as a "Detractor", meaning the customer would not necessarily recommend our services.

* A score of 9-10 is classed as a "Promoter", a customer is extremely happy with their experience and will tell other people (Promote).

* A score between 7-8 is a "Passive" score. Customer is neither happy or unhappy.

A: When a NPS Survey comes back with a score of 1-6, a Complaint case is created and email sent to the TL and ATL to advise. The customer will be emailed and advised that a Business Leader will be contacting them within 48hrs. When a NPS Survey has a score of 9-10, a Compliment case is created. No action needs to be taken with those.
